WHAT IS BHARAT QR?

Bharat QR is a standard Quick Response (QR) code platform, developed as part of Digital India Initiative. It lets merchant use one QR code to accept payments from customers holding card with any payment network.

HOW DOES IT HELP ME?

Now, there is no need to swipe your card or enter PINs while waiting in a queue. Just shop or pay bills, scan the QR code on your IndusMobile app and with a One Time Password, the payment will be done.

Go ahead and experience simple and convenient payments.
