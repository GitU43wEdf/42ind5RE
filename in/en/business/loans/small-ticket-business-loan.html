Overview

With our small ticket business loan, get quick and easy access to finance and fulfill short and medium term fund requirements of your business.

Become a smart business owner and access pre-qualified business loan in just a few clicks with digital loan application and quick disbursal.

Features

  • Loan amount of Rs. 2 to Rs. 20 lakhs
  • Loan tenure of 12 to 36 months
  • For business purposes to fulfill short and medium term fund requirements
  • Easy documentation and speedy processing
  • No collateral required

Eligibility

Type of constitution: Self-employed individuals/ sole proprietor small enterprises consisting of manufacturers, retail and wholesale trade and services customers

Business Vintage: Minimum 2 years and above in same line of business

Age: The applicant should be atleast 25 years at the time of applying for the loan, and should be no older than 70 years at the time of loan maturity

GST: For loans greater than Rs. 5 Lakhs, GST is required
